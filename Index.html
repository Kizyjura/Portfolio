About Me

Welcome to my digital space. I'm passionate about cooking, code, music, and storytelling. Born in '94 i was on the verge of the digital revolution. i grew up learning and adapting to a fast paced world that made my learning process more fluid and prone to adapting to new means of work and information. Since i was a child, i have always been fascinated by the intersection of creativity and technology. My journey has taken me through various fields, from web development to cooking, and now I find joy in blending these passions into unique projects. Whether I'm crafting a website, whipping up a new recipe, or composing music, I strive to bring a touch of creativity and innovation to everything I do. My goal is to inspire others to explore their own creative paths and to show that with a little imagination, anything is possible.

Feel free to explore my portfolio, check out my skills, and connect with me. I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations that allow me to grow and share my passions. Let's create something amazing together!
